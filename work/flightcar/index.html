FlightCar iOS Design

Product design, visual design, user research, animations

View in the app store

FlightCar, a company that helps people rent out their cars while they’re on vacation, came to thoughtbot for a complete visual update to their first iOS app. The app posed some unique communications and design challenges as a two-sided market, with users either renting cars or listing their own cars for rent, all taking place near airports across the United States.

Built by FlightCar’s fantastic mobile developers, the functionality of the app was mostly finished and their team had been working on design fixes along the way. Without a designer in-house, they had already identified the need for a designer to come into the project and create “visual polish” and generally update the look and feel of the app, but weren’t sure how to direct design decisions beyond that.

I served as the only designer on the project. With only a three-week window before needing to submit to the app store and implementation happening simultaneously, I decided to step back and evaluate the best approach to the problem.
